== Description ==

**Recipe AI Automator** is the ultimate solution for food bloggers and culinary entrepreneurs who want to scale their content production with quality and speed.

Stop wasting hours writing recipes and hunting for images. This plugin connects your WordPress site to the world's most advanced AI models (ChatGPT, Gemini, and DALL-E 3) to create professional, high-retention recipe posts with a single click.

> **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key):** To keep costs low and power high, you use your own OpenAI or Google Gemini API keys. No middleware, no extra subscriptions.

### Key Features:
*   **Smart Content Factory:** Generates full recipes with SEO headings, narrative introduction (Mary's Style), ingredients table, step-by-step instructions, and FAQs.
*   **Gourmet Image Generation:** Fully integrated with DALL-E 3 to create high-quality, professional food photography optimized for Google Discover.
*   **SEO Auto-Pilot:** Automatically populates focus keywords and meta descriptions for popular SEO plugins (like SEO Engine Pro, Yoast, and RankMath).
*   **Nutrition Data E-E-A-T:** Complete nutrition table calculation to boost your authority and search rankings.
*   **Keyword Queue Management:** Bulk process your keyword ideas into published posts.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `recipe-ai-automator` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to **Recipe AI -> Settings** and enter your API Keys (OpenAI or Gemini).
4. Start adding keywords to your queue and watch the magic happen!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin include AI credits? =
No. You use your own API keys. This ensures you pay the lowest possible price directly to the AI providers.

= Is the content unique? =
Yes! We use advanced prompting techniques to ensure every recipe has a unique narrative style and structure.
